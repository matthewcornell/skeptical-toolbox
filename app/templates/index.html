Description

This is a simple proof of concept web app that attempts to implement the ideas in Proposal: An online skeptical toolbox. The idea is simple: Enter the URL of a news article or blog post whose trustworthiness you'd like to evaluate, hit Analyze, and see a host of results that you can use to help form your opinion on the veracity of the writing.

To try it on some timely examples:

  • reddit.com/r/skeptic
  • news.google.com/news
  • foxnews.com

The GitHub project is at github.com/matthewcornell/skeptical-toolbox
